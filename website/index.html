Meet Dr. Chandra "Joy" Dunn

Transformative Leader in AI-Powered Project Management

Chandra Dunn, PMP, PhD is a transformative leader at the intersection of technology, data engineering, and project management. As founder of JoyofPM AI Solutions, Chandra builds custom AI-powered project management solutions that eliminate administrative burden and restore strategic focus to project leaders. Her startup specializes in deploying intelligent automation using AWS services like Bedrock and Lambda, creating solutions that automate status reporting, predict project risks, and streamline workflows while maintaining enterprise-grade security and client data ownership.

With a unique combination of academic rigor and hands-on technical expertise, Chandra holds a PhD in International Affairs from American University and has earned multiple AWS certifications, including Solutions Architect Associate, AI Practitioner, and Cloud Practitioner. This rare blend of analytical depth and cloud architecture skills enables her to design scalable, secure solutions that solve real business problems.

Proven Track Record

At the Massachusetts Department of Transportation, Chandra led a $2.7M road test system implementation, delivering it 2 months ahead of schedule and $500K under budget, while achieving a 30% reduction in customer wait times.

Her track record includes supporting over $25M in technology transformations, always with an eye toward making systems more efficient and user-friendly.

Technical Excellence

As a Technical Documentation & Training Specialist, Chandra creates comprehensive technical materials that bridge the gap between complex cloud architectures and diverse audiences.

Through JoyofPM AI Solutions, she implements custom solutions in clients' AWS accounts with transparent pricing and complete ownership, typically reducing routine PM work by 40-70%.

YouTube Channel: Joy of Project Management

Chandra's passion for AI-powered innovation extends to her YouTube channel, where she demonstrates how artificial intelligence is transforming the project management landscape. Through hands-on demos and reviews of leading AI platforms, she shows project managers, consultants, and forward-thinking professionals how to automate reporting, predict risks, streamline workflows, and escape repetitive tasks.

Her content focuses on reducing administrative burden, enabling data-driven decision-making, and enhancing creativity and teamwork through intelligent automation—bringing the "joy" back to project management one AI-powered solution at a time.

Journey of Continuous Learning

Chandra's journey reflects a commitment to continuous learning and reinvention. From her early career as a Senior Lecturer at the University of Tennessee to her current work building production-grade data pipelines for under $1/month using AWS, she demonstrates that persistent problem-solving and strategic skill development can open new pathways in any career.

"Embrace the learning curve. Strategic career transitions require patience, but combining your existing strengths with new technical skills creates a competitive advantage that is hard to replicate. Most importantly, focus on solving problems that matter—when your work creates real impact and value for clients, the technical challenges become opportunities for growth rather than obstacles."

Beyond Work

Outside of work, Chandra enjoys walking, playing tennis and soccer, and finds clarity and renewal hiking the trails of New England, reflecting her deep love of nature. She also channels her curiosity into personal data and AI projects, constantly exploring new ways cloud architecture can transform how we work.
